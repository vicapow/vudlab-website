VUDlab @ UC Berkeley




The past decade has witnessed explosive growth in urban populations and digital records. The Visualizing Urban Data Idealab (VUDlab) builds on both trends by teaching and inventing techniques to clearly evoke urban data. The IdeaLab hosts speakers, teaches software and computer languages, and encourages collaboration.

The VUDlab is brand new (as of May 2013), so we're still getting up and running. Fall 2013 is when things will really kick off.
